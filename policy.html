we are committed to respecting and protecting the privacy of our customers.

Privacy is a vital attribute to which we at Hubb Central Services Inc. treat our customers' data, as confidentiality is one the main objects of the company. As with any existing company in accordance with the laws of the Barbados and we are committed to respecting and protecting the privacy of our users.

Reasons for collecting data:

  1. To ease the process of conducting transactions. Providing an online portal for consumers to conduct transactions, makes it easier and faster for consumers to conduct business with our company.
  2. Direct communication. Collecting information such as the consumers' email, phone number and name, allows for frequently asked questions' responses to be done similar to in person but in a less physical contact environment.
We collect data that is only necessary for the purposes mentioned above.
Hubb Central Services Inc. offers a variety of services. It is our policy to respect and protect the privacy of our users. This privacy policy applies as soon as you visit the Hubb Central Services Inc. website for the first time.
By browsing the Hubb Central Services Inc. website, the user/visitor consents to the use of their personal information in accordance with the policies described in this Privacy Policy.

collections of information

Some users/visitors to the website choose to interact with Hubb Central Services Inc.'s website in ways that requires it to collect minimal and negligible information and storage it to the browser for a sleek experience when using the website. For example, we ask users/visitors to select a service, when they do a checkout pop up is displayed. When this is displayed, data is stored to the browser so that when or if the page is refreshed, the browser keeps the pop up displayed; however, not recording any data entered by the user/visitor. If data is saved, it is not on the part of the website visited, but the browser used. Those who engage in transactions with Hubb Central Services Inc., are asked to provide additional information to by an external service, FYGARO, which allows for the online transaction to take place. Visitors may refuse to provide personally identifiable information, and opt to conduct business in person.

If we decide to make any material changes to our Privacy Policies, we will notify you by posting an updated version of the Policies in this section or by posting it via our social medias - Recruiting Hubb on Facebook, recruitinghubb on Instagram and recruitinghubb on Tik Tok or informing you on the home page or other sections of the site to keep you updated of the changes made. refunding

At Hubb Central Services Inc. we have a non-refundable policy in place.

minors
